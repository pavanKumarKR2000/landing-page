About us

CredoKey SoftTech Pvt. Ltd. located in Silicon Valley of India, Bengaluru, is a software solutions provider working in the field of EduTech and FinTech. Established in the year 2021, has one of the most popular websites providing diversified content to its customer. The company is also one of the innovative software providers for education institutions, to automate most of the activities carried out at colleges or universities. Finance is core of any organization or individual,CredoKey SoftTech Pvt. Ltd. Provides one of the best self-help financial planner for individuals. The company not only offers software solution to individuals, but also has best in the class financial management software for organizations.

What we do

Our solutions enable students to learn on their own pace and gather information required for them for self-learning. We harnessing the power of hyper-automation and cloud to bring to our customers adapt and transform their education delivery to digital platform. Self-helped financial software aims at providing greater insights with help of machine learning and data analytics to help an individual grow financially step-by-step and day-by-day.
